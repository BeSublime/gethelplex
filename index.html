About Code for Boston

Code for Boston is a volunteer group of civic-minded developers, designers, urban planners, citizen activists, data geeks and more. Part of the Code for America Brigade program, we work with Boston-area city governments and local communities to build open-source civic apps, open public data sources, and generally help to leverage technology for use in the public sphere.

Feel free to stop by one of our Weekly Hack Nights, check us out on Github, or simply drop us a line!
